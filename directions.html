 Driving Directions to:

SS. Peter and Paul Russian Orthodox Church
 159 Stiles Street, Elizabeth, New Jersey 07208


From the north: Garden State Parkway south to exit 140B. On exit ramp stay in right lane, make first right (marked Elizabeth & Rt. 82 east), come down to the main road and turn right on Morris Avenue (Rt. 82). Go 3 miles, pass Kean University, continue past intersection of North Avenue. At the second street on the right (pizza place and fruit stand on corners), turn right on to Stiles Street. The church is in the second block on the right.

From the south: Garden State Parkway north to exit 137. Come down ramp and turn left on to Westfield Avenue (Rt. 28). Go 3 miles, look for White Castle on right corner at intersection of Elmora Avenue. Continue straight through intersection, then make first left on to Stiles Street. The church is in the third block on the left.


From Staten Island: Take the Goethals Bridge, as you come off bridge take first exit to the right, stay in left lane and make left turn at the light. Turn rught at next light on to Bayway Avenue. Go to the traffic circle, go half way around the circle and continue straight on Elmora Avenue. Continue about 15 blocks to Westfield Avenue (White Castle on left, Exxon station on right). Turn right on to Westfield Avenue, then make the first left on to Stiles Street. The church is in the third block on the left.
