Easter Picnic: Joy of Christ's Resurrection!

Dear brothers and sisters!

The bright and joyful holiday of Christ's Resurrection is approaching, and we invite everyone to share this joy together at our Easter picnic! Bring and share your Easter culinary creations: dyed eggs, Easter cakes, cottage cheese Easter desserts!

Date & Time: May 5th, 10:00 a.m.

Location: 154 Stiles Street, Elizabeth, NJ 07208

Event program includes music, contests, "Easy Quizzy" quiz, fun, and festive mood for everyone!

Contribution for food: $40 for adults and $20 for children over 7 - transfer to our church's PayPal.
