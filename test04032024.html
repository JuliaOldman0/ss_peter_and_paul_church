Dear brothers and sisters!

Dear members of our church community,

It is with deep sorrow that we announce the passing of Archpriest Emil Minkovich, the rector emeritus of the Church of the Holy Apostles Peter and Paul in Elizabeth, New Jersey.

The farewell of Father Emil will take place on Friday and Saturday, April 5th and 6th.

On Friday, the church will be open for visitation starting at 4pm, with the funeral service commencing at 7pm.

On Saturday: - 10:00am - Divine Liturgy.

Memory eternal to Father Emil!


 

 

 
 

OUR CHURCH SERVICES IN THE TIME OF COVID-19

While the church is open for services according to the published schedule, we continue to follow directives to wear masks and  maintain social-distancing.  When we pray before icons , we should only bow, but not kiss them.  There will be no kissing  of the cross at the end of Liturgy, but veneration of the icon on the Tetrapod.  Those receiving Holy Communion will follow the directions by Fr. Emil.  The Antidoron will be in individual cups.  We are making every effort to sanitize contact areas before and after services, but you should still use hand sanitizer at the candle counter as you leave.  Parishioners who do not feel well, or are not ready to come out should continue to pray at home.
